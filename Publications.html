Publications - Sushma Chowdary

QR code encryption using LU decomposition and PCA

  • A robust encryption approach for QR codes unlike the traditional byte-encryption this employs matrix-based transformations to secure data that uses LU Decomposition breaks down data into simpler parts, while PCA reduces its dimensionality by eliminating unnecessary information.

  • By combining these above methods in the encryption process and introducing secure key integration at multiple stages, it ensures that only authenticated persons can access the information of the QR code.
